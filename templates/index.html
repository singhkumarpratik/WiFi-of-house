Frequently Asked Questions

What does this do?

It is a simple web application which allows you to share your WiFi credentials instantly with your friends and family.

How does this work?

Our application generates a PDF file of a QR code, which contains your WiFi credentials. You can take a printout of the PDF file. Everyone can then simply scan the QR code to instantly connect to your WiFi.

Do you store our WiFi credentials in your database?

No, We do not store your WiFi credentials or any other kind of information about you.
